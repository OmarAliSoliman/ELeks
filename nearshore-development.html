Nearshore vs offshore vs onshore
When you need to scale or skill-up resources for a project, onshore development offers an effective but expensive solution. An offshore team saves you time and money but lacks some of the control.

Partnering with a nearshore development company is a winning solution. Nearshoring offers a middle ground where you can benefit from cost efficiency without sacrificing quality.

With an ELEKS nearshore software development team, you get the best of both worlds: a team that’s located close enough for streamlined communication and onsite visits, with the efficiency and cost savings of offshore development.
