How we do it

We follow the Double Diamond design model to explore the problem, define the challenges then propose and test ideas that pinpoint the best sustainable business solution for your needs.

  • Conducting initial research

    Our team will carry out comprehensive market research, surveys and interviews with your key stakeholders to create a clear picture of your organisation. That way we can understand your challenges and needs and help you define the goals for your solution.

  • Defining opportunity areas

    Based on the challenge overview, our findings and the needs you’ve prioritised, we’ll create a focused vision of the opportunity areas that will give you the best return, so you can reach your green-business goals with optimal investment.

  • Developing business cases

    We’ll move on to selecting and developing three high-level business cases for each of the areas you’ve prioritised, taking into consideration factors such as desirability, feasibility and viability.

  • Crafting your architecture vision

    Once you’ve approved the business case, we’ll proceed with the architecture design. Our software experts will identify tech stacks and hardware components that meet your specific requirements and work within your business context. We can then select and allocate a project team that’s best equipped to deliver it.

  • Presenting the outcomes

    Following ELEKS’ business consulting, you’ll receive a final report for building your sustainability strategy, including a holistic description and in-depth analysis of business opportunities or technology areas, business case development and prioritisation, implementation roadmaps, adoption timelines and budget proposals.
