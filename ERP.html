ELEKS’ ERP software consulting services
  • ERP system/platform selection

    ERP solutions are an essential component of effective modern enterprise resource planning. ELEKS' specialist team of ERP consultants has comprehensive industry experience and the expertise to guide you through the process of choosing and implementing the best-fit ERP solution for your business.

  • Fit-gap analysis

    We’ll meticulously analyse your business processes and data to identify where ERP functionality could optimise your operations. If customisation or application development services are needed, our multi-skilled enterprise software experts are on hand to offer end-to-end project support.

  • ABAP services

    If standard SAP solutions can’t fully support your enterprise's evolving business operations, we’ll help you introduce essential adjustments and customisation using ABAP language. Our team of ERP consultants possesses tried-and-tested ABAP programming expertise, a deep understanding of enterprise business processes and extensive multi-domain knowledge. With our ERP consulting services, you can make the most of a system that’s perfectly tuned to your organisation.

  • ERP implementation

    From process design to post-go-live support, the ELEKS team is ready to provide full-cycle ERP consulting and implementation services. We have over 30 years of enterprise software development experience and a successful track record of enterprise projects in multiple industries, including finance, insurance, retail, logistics, energy and many more.

  • ERP quality assurance & system performance testing

    Our comprehensive quality assurance process will guarantee your ERP's flawless performance by discovering and rectifying any problems and gaps associated with the system's implementation. Our team provides effective resolutions and optimisations for any issues discovered, so you can ensure maximum performance, robust security and steadfast adherence to all industry standards and best practices.

  • BI/BO implementation

    SAP BusinessObjects BI is a comprehensive suite of applications that allows you to view, analyse and manage your business intelligence data. We’ll help you adopt modern BI and BO functionality, so you can start generating more value from the data you have. Leverage advanced predictive analytics to drive decision-making, apply complex queries and create reports to get actionable insights, all while benefitting from convenient data-visualisation dashboards in one integrated ERP solution.

  • System integration

    Our team of experts will help you seamlessly integrate your ERP with all of your enterprise IT ecosystem’s other business applications, including e-commerce platforms, CRM, marketing tools and other databases and data points. So, you can enable straightforward data input and output, secure data exchange and streamlined communication between all systems.

  • Post-implementation review

    For existing ERPs that are hard to work with, suffer performance issues or simply don't meet your organisation's needs, a post-implementation review by an experienced team of enterprise experts is essential. We’ll help evaluate, test and fix currently deployed ERP systems, and, for all ERPs we implement, we’ll perform post-implementation reviews to ensure the system flawlessly integrates with your environment and meets your expectations.
